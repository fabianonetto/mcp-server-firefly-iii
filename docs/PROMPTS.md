# Firefly III AI Prompt Gallery (v2.0.0)

This guide provides example prompts for all 47 tools available in the AI Bridge.

## 💰 Account Management
- *"Create a new asset account called 'Cash Wallet' with a $50 opening balance."*
- *"Close my old 'Savings Account' (set active to false)."*
- *"Delete the testing account with ID 123."*
- *"List all my liability accounts."*

## 💸 Transactions & Spending
- *"I spent $15.50 on lunch at Chipotle using my 'Checking Account'."*
- *"Transfer $200 from 'Savings' to 'Checking'."*
- *"Update the description of my last transaction to 'Monthly Rent (Corrected)'."*
- *"Show me transaction ID 456 in detail."*

## 📊 Budgets & Organization
- *"Create a new budget called 'Subscriptions'."*
- *"Am I over budget on 'Groceries'?"*
- *"Create a new category for 'Home Office' and tag it as 'Taxable'."*
- *"Move my last Amazon purchase to the 'Gifts' category."*

## 📅 Bills & Recurring
- *"Do I have any bills coming up this week?"*
- *"Record a new monthly bill for 'Electricity' of about $100."*
- *"List all my recurring transaction rules."*

## 🧠 Intelligence & Automation
- *"Trigger my 'Nightly Cleanup' rule group."*
- *"Create a new rule that tags anything from 'Starbucks' as #Coffee."*
- *"Show me my current automation rules."*
- *"Get details for Rule Group ID 5."*

## 🐷 Savings (Piggy Banks)
- *"I have 5 piggy banks. Show me how close I am to reaching my 'New Car' goal."*
- *"Put $100 into my 'Emergency Fund'."*
- *"Create a new piggy bank called 'Holiday Trip' with a target of $2000."*

## 📁 Attachments & Insights
- *"Upload this receipt to my last transaction."* (Requires file path/Base64)
- *"Show me my balance trend chart for the last 6 months."*
- *"List all files I have uploaded."*

## ⚙️ System & Preferences
- *"Check our connection to Firefly III."*
- *"Change my system preference for the dashboard view to 'classic'."*
- *"What is my current default currency?"*
