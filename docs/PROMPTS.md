# Firefly III AI Prompt Gallery

This guide provides example prompts you can use to interact with your Firefly III instance via Gemini, Claude, or ChatGPT.

## 💰 Accounts & Balances
- *"What is my total net worth across all asset accounts?"*
- *"Show me the current balance of my 'Checking Account'."*
- *"Do I have any accounts with a negative balance?"*
- *"List all my expense accounts."*

## 💸 Transactions & Spending
- *"Record a $45.00 withdrawal for 'Grocery Shopping' from my 'Checking Account' to 'Whole Foods'."*
- *"I just received my salary of $3000. Record it as a deposit into my 'Savings Account' from 'My Company'."*
- *"Show me my last 5 transactions."*
- *"Transfer $100 from 'Checking' to 'Savings'."*

## 📊 Budgets & Goals
- *"How much is left in my 'Entertainment' budget for this month?"*
- *"List all my active budgets and their status."*
- *"Am I over budget on 'Dining Out'?"*
- *"How close am I to reaching my 'New Laptop' savings goal?"*
- *"Add $50 to my 'Emergency Fund' piggy bank."*

## 🔍 Discovery & Search
- *"Find all transactions from last week that were over $100."*
- *"Search for any transactions involving 'Amazon'."*
- *"How much did I spend at 'Starbucks' in total last month?"*
- *"Show me all transactions tagged as 'Vacation'."*

## 🏷️ Organization
- *"List all my transaction categories."*
- *"Create a new category called 'Tech Subscriptions'."*
- *"What are my most used tags?"*
- *"Create a new tag called 'Tax Deductible'."*

## 📅 Bills & Recurring
- *"Do I have any bills due this week?"*
- *"List all my recurring transaction rules."*
- *"What is the status of my 'Rent' bill?"*

---
**Tip**: When creating transactions, you can be specific or conversational. For example: *"I spent 5 bucks on a coffee at Dunkin"* will be translated into a withdrawal from your default wallet to a destination called 'Dunkin'.
